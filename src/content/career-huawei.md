## Huawei Malaysia — Management Trainee

**Until 2021.** A structured rotational program at Huawei's Malaysian subsidiary, anchored in the **B2B Channel Sales department** — my first role inside a global technology MNC.

<span class="tag">B2B Sales</span> <span class="tag">Channel Sales</span> <span class="tag">Pre-sales</span> <span class="tag">Tender Management</span> <span class="tag">Stakeholder Management</span> <span class="tag">Cross-functional</span>

The rotation gave me a grounded view of the commercial side of an enterprise product cycle — sales-led, but cross-functional in practice. Most weeks bridged product, supply chain, and customer-facing work for partners across the Malaysian channel.

### 🛠 What I worked on

#### Product Presentation & Sales Enablement
- Prepared **presentation decks** and **product price lists** for client-facing meetings.
- Pitched to prospective clients and led **product demonstrations** during sales engagements.
- Delivered **product training sessions** to clients' internal sales teams on newly launched lines.
- Hosted **webinars** introducing new product launches to prospective channel partners.
- Handled queries on product features, configurations, and use cases.

#### Proposals & Tender Submissions
- Matched Huawei's product portfolio against **client requirements** to recommend the right configuration.
- Drafted submissions for **tender projects** — both the technical and commercial sections.
- Drove **cross-functional alignment** with internal teams to confirm proposed products were available, deliverable on time, and accurately scoped.

#### Order Processing & Partner Onboarding
- Issued **product quotations** to prospective clients.
- Processed **purchase orders (POs)** and issued the corresponding invoices.
- Coordinated **stock delivery scheduling** with clients and internal logistics for on-time fulfilment.
- Onboarded new business partners into Huawei's **internal customer-registration systems**.

When the contract wrapped in 2021, I made the deliberate move out of the commercial track and into data — enrolling in the MSc Data Science & Business Analytics at APU later that year.
