This is the first post on my new blog. I used to write on WordPress; this site is the rebuild.

## What I'll write about

Mostly the work I do day to day, distilled into something useful for anyone walking the same path:

- **Pipelines, in detail.** The patterns we settle on for SFTP, BigQuery, Postgres, and Airflow — including the trade-offs that aren't obvious until you've shipped a few.
- **LLMs in production.** Where they actually move the needle on internal tooling, where they don't, and what it took to get them past prototype.
- **The pivot.** Coming from a business background into data engineering — what transferred, what I had to relearn, and what I'd tell someone making the same jump today.

## What you can expect

Short, specific posts grounded in real work. No 3,000-word think pieces. If a post can't survive the question "would this help someone shipping next week?", it doesn't go up.
